Introduction to Tenses in English

  • What are Tenses? Tenses show the time of an action or event.
  • Types of Tenses:
    • Past Tense: Describes actions that already happened.
      • Example: "I walked to school yesterday."
    • Present Tense: Describes actions that are currently happening or habits.
      • Example: "She eats breakfast every day."
    • Future Tense: Describes actions that will happen in the future.
      • Example: "They will visit tomorrow."
  • Importance of Using Correct Tense:
    • Clear communication of ideas.
    • Correct grammar and sentence structure.
    • Better understanding in conversation and writing.
